Back to Win / Loss ↑

Win / Loss Debrief

Capture clear feedback after every closed opportunity so the team can improve positioning, proposal quality, pricing, and competitive strategy.

Required at Stage 8

1. Why did you choose CTM?

Capture the primary value drivers, differentiators, relationship factors, service strengths, technology, pricing, or proof points that influenced the decision.

2. Which supplier did you choose?

If CTM was not selected, document the selected competitor/supplier and any known reasons for the decision.

3. How well was the proposal prepared?

Rate proposal quality, clarity, accuracy, responsiveness, compliance, visuals, and whether the proposal addressed the client’s key needs.

4. What could CTM improve?

Document gaps in solution fit, pricing, timing, stakeholder engagement, presentation, implementation confidence, or proof points.

5. What influenced the final decision?

Capture decision criteria such as service model, cost, global coverage, technology, reporting, traveler experience, account management, and implementation plan.

6. What follow-up actions are needed?

Assign owner, due date, CRM update, competitive intelligence update, client follow-up, and content improvement actions.

CRM Stage Flow

Use the cards below as a visual guide for moving opportunities through the sales cycle.

0 Lead Generation / Prospecting

Assigned territory, dormant account, contacts identified, nurture or marketing list.

1 Active

Need and interest established, prospect contact made, initial intro call scheduled.

2 Discovery / Qualification

Goals shared, pain points identified, next meeting scheduled, recap sent and confirmed.

3 Solution Development Engagement

Key players accessed, solution aligned, buying process understood, relationship map created.

4 Proposal Submitted

RFP/RFI received, go/no-go complete, bid requirements captured, proposal submitted.

5 Presentation Stage

Stakeholder presentation completed or sent, solutions aligned, follow-up tracked.

6 Short Listed / Negotiations

Shortlisted, clarifications active, B&F pricing discussions, OTL review, or contract draft sent.

7 Verbal Award

Business requested, verbal approval granted, written intent to award, and redlining underway.

8 Closed - Won / Loss

Formal contract signed, confirmed loss, no decision, or no activity after six months.
